Conference Details

NEXT > Create. Innovate. Repeat.

April 29-30, 2019 @ London, Ontario

NEXT> create.innovate.repeat. is a two days conference held in beautiful London, Ontario. It brings together educators, artists, engineers, from leading positions across the Globe. The conference offers a full immersion in fields of art and creative technology. NEXT> is an opportunity to discover new trends and new potential to apply technology and creativity; a great way to learn, to be in touch, and to see new opportunities we will have to interact with innovations in the near future.

Let's create the unexpected!

Conference Topics

Main Topics covered in the conference:

Sound Sculptures. Hands-on experience in mixing, sound design and digital audio. Interaction with old law of physics and new waves of algorithms.

Machine Learning - AI. Artificial Intelligence applied to art. New creativity instruments that can be used with gestures. Understanding the pipeline of models and signals to create the ultimate interaction.

Audio Visual Art. Multisensorial exploration of sounds and colors. Softwares influences all aspects of our visual and audio culture. Creative direction in various environments and ideas.

Virtual Reality. Modelling and interacting with virtual reality. A new array of tools and experiences that can be applied in any fields. Creations and features ready to be developed and explored.
