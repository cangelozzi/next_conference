About Us

Next> create.innovate.repeat. is an unique opportunity to promote and highlight new processes and tools that bring technology and creativity to the next level of interaction. Dedicated and passionate people will have a chance to meet, talk, show and learn.

For this first-edition, Next> will be in London, Ontario, chosen for its flourishing IT sector and vibe; a growing community of researchers developers and designers will be exposed to the latest technologies and trends applied to creativity. An important opportunity to network, to be exposed and to learn from academic contributors and simple passionates.

Learning, create and innovate has to be a great opportunity for a vast majority of people, that is why tickets prices are kept as low as possible for everyone; in addition, free live streams of events will be offered online, together with talks and highlights which will be available few days after the venue.

No excuse, get prepared and come on visit us to create, innovate and share the future.
